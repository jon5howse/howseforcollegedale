## Design Decisions

Adopted the guiding philosophy:

**Working Together for Collegedale**

Established the following design principles:

* Community before politics.
* Transparency through evidence.
* Residents first.
* Mobile-first design.
* Explain government clearly.
* Photography tells the story.
* Build once. Reuse everywhere.

---

## Architectural Decisions

Project Beacon is organized around resident questions rather than government departments.

The platform supplements the official City of Collegedale website instead of replacing it.

Official government services will continue to be provided through the City's website.

Project Beacon focuses on:

* Community engagement
* Public communication
* Project transparency
* Commission understanding
* Community CARE meetings
* Laura's documented public service
